Blessed to Hunt is a Christian bowhunting nonprofit born from the belief that God's creation is the perfect backdrop for growing closer to Him and to one another.

Where Scripture Meets the Stand

Blessed to Hunt is an outdoor ministry and faith-based hunting organization that brings together men, women, and youth who share a love for bowhunting and a heart for God's Word. As a Christian archery group based in Cincinnati, Ohio, we believe that some of the deepest conversations about faith happen when you're surrounded by His creation.

Every Saturday morning, our members gather for Bible study and archery practice — building skills with a bow while building a deeper relationship with Christ and each other. Whether you call it a hunting fellowship, an outdoor ministry, or just church in the woods, everyone is welcome.

What We Do

Faith, Fellowship & the Field

Our chapters gather weekly for three core activities designed to strengthen your walk with God and sharpen your skills in the field.

📖

Bible Study

Dig into God's Word together every Saturday morning. Our studies are designed for hunters — real talk, real questions, and real application for everyday life.

🏹

Archery Practice

Sharpen your shooting after study. Whether you're picking up a bow for the first time or fine-tuning your form, all skill levels are welcome.

🌤

Community Hunts

Throughout the season, we organize group hunts and outdoor fellowship events. Nothing beats sharing a hunt camp with brothers and sisters in Christ.
